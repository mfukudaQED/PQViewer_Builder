Help / shortcuts note
Shortcuts: Shift+A normally views the opposite a-axis. If the pivot marker is selected, Shift+A adds an atom at the pivot and enters Grab. The plot area initially extends to the bottom of the viewport; drag the blue corner to resize it independently from the panels. During Grab, Shift+A remains the opposite a-axis view shortcut. Shift+D duplicates selected atoms in-place and enters Grab. Delete removes selected atoms. x/y/z and a/b/c are view shortcuts.
